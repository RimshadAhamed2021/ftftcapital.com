About us

FTFT Capital Investments LLC is a UAE based financial technology company investing in blockchain technology companies.

FTFT Capital Investments is a subsidiary of the New York based FinTech Giant, the Future Fintech Group Inc. listed on Nasdaq. FT FT Capital Investments was founded in 2021 with its headquarter in Dubai, UAE. The fintech holding company has embarked on an adventure to pursue investments in various fintech subsidiaries by introducing its unconventional technology and expertise to drive innovation and growth in the digital payment ecosystem
